Description

Mr Nibbles dreams of becoming the first hamster in space. Using unique tilt-and-tap controls help him escape from his cage prison then navigate the perilous backyard before blasting off into space. Along the way collect nibbles and avoid spiders in this challenging platformer. Spin the wheel for the chance to win powerups and new outfits with unique graphics, effects and sounds.

History

After the succes of our earlier title Mr Nibbles in 2011 we knew we wanted to make another game about Mr Nibbles. We didn’t want to make a simple sequel instead we asked ourselves what would Mr Nibbles look like if it was an endless runner instead of a puzzle platformer? An experiment or two later after the core gameplay had been proven to work Mr Nibbles Forever was born.

Features

  • 3 different stages, cage, backyard and orbit
  • 13 different outfits to collect each with unique graphics, effects and sounds
  • 2 powerups to win
  • Exciting tilt and tap controls
  • 14 different Challenges to test your skills on and earn prizes
  • Compete for the daily, weekly and alltime highscores

About Epic Shrimp

Epic Shrimp was formed in 2015 and serves as the home the various recent games by Michael Cann along with his friends and collaborators. Michael has been a professional games developer for 8+ years but has been a passionate games maker since he was 7 years old. Since the early days of visual basic, through the Flash years and into the modern Mobile App era he has always strived to entertain people with his creations.
