About

Epic Shrimp

We are a collection of professional and non-professional games developers who have teamed up to create some epic games.

The Shrimps

Mike ‘Mantis Shrimp’ Cann (Coder)

Mike has worked for over 10 years in the games industry on various titles from the old J2ME Worms games up to the latest multi-million-player social games. Recently he decided to team up with some of his developer friends and conquer the world with epic games.

Laura ‘Ninja Shrimp’ Whyte (Artist)

Laura has worked in the games industry for many years as a 2D artist and animator. She was responsible for all the awesome paper-craft artwork on Ectoplasm.

Joe ‘Skeleton Shrimp’ Watkinson (Audio)

Joe is a recent member of the Epic Shrimp club but has more than proven that he has got what it takes with his excellent work on Mr Nibbles Forever.

Woody ‘Ghost Shrimp’ Stables (Artist)

An artist living and working in the UK, Woody loves to tackle a challenge and produce assets for cool, fun projects like mr Nibbles
